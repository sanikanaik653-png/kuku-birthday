To my best, cute, and handsome boyfriend, happy birthday 💗

This is the day I’ve been waiting for, to tell you how I feel about you. You’re not just my boyfriend… sometimes you’ve been my best friend, sometimes like my mother, and sometimes like my father. Thank you for being all of that for me. I love being your baby.

Honestly, I’ve never felt this way for anyone before, because with you, I feel like I’m at home. I want this feeling to stay with me forever. The first half of 2025 was really bad for me, but in the second half, one beautiful thing happened — you came into my life. After that, my life became super happy, cool, and yes, sometimes a little sad too… but it’s okay, because I know you’ll always be there for me.

In the beginning, my anger issues may have hurt you — I’m really sorry for that. Now I don’t want to be angry at you anymore. You’ve always been there for me when I’m angry, sad, and even when I’m happy. I want to share all my emotions with you, because with you, I feel complete. I want to stay like this with you forever.

Everyone thinks you’re very kanjus and don’t care, but I know the truth — you spend so much and care even more. I hope you never feel that I don’t care about you, because I really do. I’m very, very happy that you are in my life.

Now I can’t even imagine my life without you 💗
